Alerting the agent of a new call

Each of these examples simulates a new call every 5 seconds. If you're window is in the background, you should see some kind of notification that the call started.

  • Subtle - The title bar flashes a message like, "You've got a call..."
  • In your face - A popup appears on top of the other browser windows.
  • In between - The taskbar item for this window flashes yellow. May bring an IE7 browser to the front.
